To calculate the corrected output, we must first establish the default material properties of the subjects when they are completely isolated from external stimuli. These are graded on a localized scale of 1.0 to 10.0.

αbase (Intrinsic Over-Investment): Subject Alpha's baseline tendency to allocate resources and emotional capital toward a zero-yield objective when unprompted.

βbase (Intrinsic Dissonance): Subject Beta's baseline rate of verbal repulsion when not actively being observed or pressured by the network.

γbase (Intrinsic Inertia): Subject Gamma's baseline mechanical failure rate—their default state of internal vocalization without physical actuation.
